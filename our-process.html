Behind the Scenes

Our Process

Process is critical to the success of digital development, but it's not a one‑size‑fits‑all exercise. Process often has to be tailored to the type of project and to the needs of our rockstars.

Part of our partnership promise is finding the right ryhthm when working together. The following provides a general framework that we employ when getting those rockstar ideas realized and launched.

Discovery

Review business objectives, identify product features, identify user stories and use cases.

Identify success criteria for all the types of users/roles who will be using the product.

Design

There is more to our design phase than what meets the eye. Our design phase includes User Interfase (UI) design, User Experience(UX) design, Software Architecture design, Hardware Architecture design, designing Quality Assurance (QA) test criteria, and desinging a project plan for the remainder of the project.

Rapid UX Prototyping

We know that seeing is believing. The rapid UX prototyping phase allows us to quickly showcase the UI and UX design direction to ensure the vision is aligned with everyone's expectations. We'll course correct as needed, until we achive 100% satisfaction from all project stakeholders.

Development and Testing

Review business objectives, identify product features, identify user stories and use case.

Identify success criteria for all types of users/roles who will be using the product.

Launch

Here is where we create a release candidate build of the software and make our final production environment configuration and implementation changes. We do final testing against this production environment and then lunch into the market. We will alse work to identify and address any issues discovered post lunch.
